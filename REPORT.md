## 5. Phase D: Engineering Risks
*Detailed failure analysis.*

1.  **Water Hammer:** Surge is **6.2 bar**. Manageable.
2.  **Biofouling:** Annual Pigging required.
3.  **Materials:** HDPE (High Density Polyethylene) is the optimal choice.
4.  **Environmental:** The thermal plume is contained within **90m** of the discharge.

## 6. Phase E: Stress Tests (The Devil's Advocate)
*Is it safe?*
